- Crazy Santa -

Play

Crazy Santa is the game where you need to collect all coins and destroy enemyes...

Controls:
to move to the left press LEFT ARROW
to move to the right press RIGHT ARROW
to jump press UP ARROW

Heroes:

For the WIN you must collect 1000 points:

-- collect star - 10 points

-- destroy enemy - 20 points

Good luck !!!
